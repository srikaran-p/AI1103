\section*{Problem}
(GATE-CS 2013 Q62) Out of all the 2-digit integers between 1 and 100, a 2-digit number has to be selected at random. What is the probability that the selected number is not divisible by 7?
\begin{enumerate}[label = (\Alph*)]
\item $\frac{13}{90}$\newline
\item $\frac{12}{90}$\newline
\item $\frac{78}{90}$ \label{option C}\newline
\item $\frac{77}{90}$
\end{enumerate}
\section*{Solution}
Let $X$ be a random variable which holds 2-digit integers.
Here, $\floor*{x}$ rounds off $x$ to the greatest integer less than $x$.
\begin{align}
    \pr{X \text{ mod } 7 \neq 0} &= 1 - \frac{n(X \text{ mod } 7 = 0)}{n(X)}\\
    \pr{X \text{ mod }7 \neq0} &= 1 -  \frac{\floor*{\frac{100}{7}}- \floor*{\frac{10}{7}}}{90} \\
    \pr{X \text{ mod }7 \neq 0} &= 1 - \frac{13}{90}\\
    \pr{X \text{ mod }7 \neq 0} &= \frac{77}{90}
\end{align}
So, the correct option is (D).
